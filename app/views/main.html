Our Aim

Edifice is a fast growing company committed to delivering the best Design, Build and Management to our customers in the field of Exhibitions, Interior and Office Furniture Fitouts. Our meticulously planned approach towards every project ensures that we are constantly delivering excellent quality within agreed deadlines and the budgetary specifications of the customer.

Mission

We take pride in the fact that each of our projects adds value to our customer’s products by the positive exposure we create. Every project is undertaken with the aim of making our client’s products and services gain maximum exposure with our creative designs. We strive to leave a long lasting impact on the end user while ensuring efficient management practices eliminate the stress for our customer during the project build.

Standards

Today, Edifice has successfully executed large projects covering more than 1300 square meters at single shows; High end stands for prestigious brands like Aston Martin and highly customized stands for Jewellery and high net worth products. Our range of experience covers automobile, real estate, jewellery, security products, defense, interior fittings, food and communication industries including cable and satellite
